CSG Calculator

What is CSG

The Customer Service Guarantee Standard (CSG Standard) protects you from poor service. It tells telcos how fast they must connect or fix your landline.

It also sets the compensation they have to pay if they miss those timelines.

What the CSG Calculator does

  • Determines CSG timeframes
  • Checks for MSD (Mass Service Disruption)
  • Calculates the total CSG payment in AUD (Australian Dollar)
